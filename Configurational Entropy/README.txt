Configurational entropy of a protein is the part of a protein's entropy that's related to the arrangement of its constituent particles. 
It can also be thought of as a measure of the disorder of the system.

####################################

REQUIREMENTS:
1. GROMACS Package installed
2. xtc file
3. pdb file

####################################

The configurational entropy can be calculated for each chain of the protein. It is calculated for:
1. Alpha carbons of the protein
2. Backbone of the protein
3. Sidechain of the protein
